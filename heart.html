Heart

what is the heart

The heart is the main organ of the circulatory system.

it is a muscular pump the size of a fist that pumps blood to all parts of the body through the blood vessels.

working non-stop and tirelessly and regulating the pulses.


function of the heart

The pumping of blood into the arteries that carries oxygen and nutrients to all tissues of the body.

Receive deoxygenated blood and metabolic waste, and pump them to the lungs to be oxygenated again.


Diseases affecting the heart

heart attack.

brain attack.

Aneurysm.

Sudden cardiac arrest.

Methods of treating the heart

Quit Smoking.

Eat healthy foods.

Control of blood pressure.

Cholesterol test.

Playing sports.
