Diabetic Disease

Diabetic

Diabetes is a chronic disease that
occurs when the pancreas does not
produce enough insulin
When the body is unable
to effectively use the insulin it produces

Symptoms

Feeling very hungry and unintended weight
loss Fatigue and weakness
Blurred vision And easy arousal
other mood changes

Methods of treatment

Sold a healthy diet Regular
exercise Weight loss
taking diabetes medications
insulin therapy Monitor blood sugar
